How this works

The inspiration for this website comes from listening to conversations at get togethers with friends and family about recent favorite wine discoveries.

Laura will tell the kids about some new wine she tried at Trader Joe's that cost $2 but tastes like it should cost $20. Maybe she took a picture of it on her phone, maybe she made a note and maybe she can't remember.

Now she can easily share this wine experience with everyone and they can share their own experiences with it, along perhaps with a picture of them drinking it or otherwise enjoying themselves.

It's a little social network centered around wine.

So login and start sharing and commenting. Note: Every wine needs a wine category and a region. You can generally get that off the bottle. If a region and wine type doesn't exist you can create one. It doesn't have to be perfect, I can fix it if it needs fixing.
